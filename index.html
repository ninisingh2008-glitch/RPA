About RPA

The official body for pickleball in Rajasthan.

The Rajasthan Pickleball Association is the recognised state body governing and developing pickleball across Rajasthan. We work alongside the Indian Pickleball Association (IPA) to build courts, certify coaches, run district leagues and select players to represent the state at national events.

Govern & Affiliate

Recognised state affiliate of the IPA. We certify officials and uphold the rules of play.

Tournaments

District opens, state championships and selection trials for the IPA Nationals — open to all skill levels.

Coaching & Clinics

Beginner clinics, junior development squads and certified-coach programs across every major city in Rajasthan.

Build Courts

Partnering with local stakeholders to add dedicated pickleball courts in every district.
